Frequently Asked Questions

How much can I borrow?

From KES 1,000 — maximum depends on your repayment history.

What interest do you charge?

30% flat interest for a 14-day loan.

What happens if I default?

Your loan will roll over for another 14 days and an additional penalty will apply (KES 300 per KES 1,000 example). Example: KES 1,000 → KES 1,300 repay; rollover → KES 1,600.

How do I repay?

Use our Repay page to pay via PayPal (or follow M-Pesa instructions where provided).
